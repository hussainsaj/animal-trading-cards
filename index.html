Kiwi

Kiwis have a strong, musty smell. Predators can smell them from faraway.

  • Family: Apterygidae
  • Class: Aves
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Kingdom: Animalia

Kiwi habits and physical characteristics are so like a mammal the bird is sometimes referred to as an honorary mammal. It has feathers like hair, nostrils at the end of its beak and an enormous egg. Most kiwi are nocturnal birds, like many of New Zealand's native animals.
